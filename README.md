# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive **Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard** developed using **Power BI**. The dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of coffee shop sales data, enabling stakeholders to monitor business performance, identify sales trends, and make data-driven decisions.

The report transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations and KPIs.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Monitor overall business performance.
- Analyze monthly sales trends.
- Compare sales across different product categories.
- Evaluate store-wise sales contribution.
- Track key business KPIs.
- Build an interactive dashboard for business decision-making.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Microsoft Excel

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

- 📈 Interactive KPI Cards
  - Total Sales
  - Total Orders
  - Total Quantity Sold
  - Average Order Value

- 📅 Monthly Sales Trend Analysis

- ☕ Product Category Sales Comparison

- 🏪 Store Location Sales Distribution

- 🎛️ Interactive Date Duration Filter

- 📌 Modern Dashboard Design with:
  - Custom Icons
  - Rounded KPI Cards
  - Professional Color Theme
  - Interactive Visualizations

---

## 📈 Key Business Insights

- Generated **Total Sales of approximately $698K**, reflecting strong business performance.

- Processed over **149K customer orders**, indicating a high transaction volume.

- Sold more than **214K products**, demonstrating consistent customer demand.

- Maintained an **Average Order Value of approximately $4.69**.

- Sales showed a positive monthly trend throughout the analysis period.

- **Coffee** emerged as the highest-performing product category, contributing the largest share of total sales.

- Lower-performing product categories present opportunities for promotional campaigns and product optimization.

- Interactive filters enable users to analyze sales by specific product categories for better business insights.
